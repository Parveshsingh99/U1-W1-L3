Recipe of focaccia with caramelized onions, cranberries, brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will became tour new favourite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups 500g all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons istant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounce brie, cut into cubes (srape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing boil, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachments, mix toghether all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need actually to knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
